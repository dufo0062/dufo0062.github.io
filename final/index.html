How Our Service Works

We Grow Them

We grow and nurture quality Balsam Fir trees on our land in Old Chelsea, Quebec, without pestisides and artificial fertilizers.

You Borrow Them

During the Christmas season, we uproot our living trees, place them in nice, big pots, and rent them to you for 2-3 weeks.
