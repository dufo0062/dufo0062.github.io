About Us

Our Background

Hello, and thank you for stopping by our website! Sustainabili-TREE was founded in 2009, by Kathryn Dufour, and Laura and Tyler Renner. We are a small, family-run business, located just outside of Ottawa, in Old Chelsea Quebec. We are dedicated to providing a sustainable Christmas tree option for home-owners in the Ottawa-Gatineau region.

Our Process

Here, at Sustainabili-TREE, we grow coniferous trees on a pesticide-free plot of land, and care for the trees year-round. Approximately two weeks before Christmas, we carefully dig up our trees, keeping their roots in tact, and place them in plant pots. We then rent these live, potted trees to interested home-owners, as an alternative to the conventional options of purchasing cut trees or artificial trees.

At the end of the Christmas season, the potted trees are then returned to our farm, and are re-planted for continued growth and future rentals. When trees become too big for in-home use, they are planted in the wild for indefinite growth and development.

Our Benefits

By renting a Christmas tree, you can actively help eliminate the plastic waste associated with artificial trees. Renting from us also helps reduce the environmental degradation associated with live, cut trees. These conventional trees are often mass-produced on monoculture farms that use pesticides.

Renting a Christmas tree eliminates the need to store an artificial tree year-round. Furthermore, since our potted trees are smaller than conventional trees, they are perfect for small living spaces.

Our services include free delivery! So save your back and your time; we will set up your Christmas tree for you.
